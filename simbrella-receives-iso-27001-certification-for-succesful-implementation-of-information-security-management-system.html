Simbrella receives ISO 27001 certification for succesful implementation of Information Security Management System

We are pleased to announce that Simbrella’s Information Security Management System implementation is ISO 27001 certified. Simbrella has successfully met ISO 27001 requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining and continually improving an information security management system.

The company has achieved this milestone by integrating information security management system with the organization’s processes and overall management structure. Information security has been considered in the design of all processes, information systems, and controls of Simbrella. Applying a risk management process, Simbrella has achieved to preserve the confidentiality, integrity and availability of information.

Our decision to work towards the ISO 27001 certification demonstrates our commitment to providing a high-quality, safe and consistent service to our clients.

Congratulations to the entire Simbrella team on the achievement!
